By indenture made at Stokelynche, on the 5th day of
March, in the 3rd year of the reign of King Henry the
Fifth; Alianor‚ widow of Walter Cloptone‚ knight‚ grants to
Nicholas Yonge, clerk, Robert Veel senior, and William
Kyngestone—All her lands and tenements in Stokelynche,

condition of the grantees paying yearly to Alianor, during her
life, 44 shillings and 4 pence of legal money, at Sylveyns (her
residenee) near Stokelynche.
